Henrietta Lacks

Henrietta Lacks died October 4, 1951. She was 31 years old.

Henrietta was an African American woman living in a small town near Baltimore. She was a homemaker and mother to 5. She died from complications due to cervical cancer. During her treatment procedure, tubes of radium were placed into her cervix to irradiate and kill the cancer cells. During this procedure, part of her tumor was cut off and sent to Dr. Gey.

Dr. Gey saw success in her cells because of how aggressive her cancer was. Henrietta also had HPV, and because of this virus, her cancer was so aggressive. Henrietta never met Dr. Gey and she did not will her body to him for medical use.
