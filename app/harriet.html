Harriet Cole

Harriet Cole died March 12, 1888. She was 36 years old.

Harriet was an African American woman living in Philadelphia. Little is known about her life. She was a domestic worker and unmarried with no children. She died of tuberculosis, which had taken a toll on her body. Her body was so afflicted by the disease that she died emaciated.

Dr. Weaver chose her body for his practice because of how little fat she died with. She was an easier cadaver to cut into. It is unlikely that Harriet knew Dr. Weaver or willed her body to him for medical use.
